## 2024-05-18 - [Avoid Redundant JSON File Reading on the Warm Path]
**Learning:** The `execute_warm_path` in `crates/rbm-ghidra/src/warm_path.rs` was unnecessarily parsing `functions.json` twice per script invocation (once to resolve the SHA-256 and again after taking a lock just to assert the project wasn't deleted while waiting). Given the high volume of tools hitting the warm path and the potential large size of `functions.json` for complex binaries, redundant disk reads and JSON parsing creates a noticeable bottleneck.
**Action:** When holding a lock to operate on an existing project, verify presence with lightweight filesystem operations like `tokio::fs::try_exists(&project_dir)` instead of parsing large metadata envelopes unless the content of the envelope is actually needed by the subsequent logic.
